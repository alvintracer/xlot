took Wallet Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

TRAVERSE CO.,Ltd ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") provides the took Wallet browser extension (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we process, why we process it, and how users may contact us regarding privacy requests.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to the took Wallet browser extension and related wallet services.

2. Information We Process

We limit data processing to what is reasonably necessary to provide wallet functionality.

  • Account and authentication information: such as email address or login identifiers, when needed for wallet sign-in, account recovery, or security verification.
  • Wallet-related information: such as public wallet addresses and transaction request metadata needed to provide wallet connection, signing, and account display features.
  • Device or technical information: such as basic device/session identifiers and extension settings needed for security, fraud prevention, session continuity, and service operation.
  • Encrypted backup data: if a user chooses wallet backup or recovery features, encrypted key material or encrypted backup fragments may be stored in encrypted form to support recovery. We do not claim access to decrypted private keys through this backup storage alone.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide wallet connection, authentication, signing, and account-related features.
  • To maintain security, prevent unauthorized access, and support account recovery.
  • To store user-selected settings and maintain extension functionality.
  • To respond to support requests and service-related inquiries.
  • To comply with applicable law, lawful requests, or security obligations where required.

4. Website Access and On-Page Data

The extension may interact with webpages that users choose to visit in order to provide wallet connectivity to decentralized applications and related web services. We do not sell browsing data, and we do not use webpage information for advertising purposes.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers only where reasonably necessary to operate the Service, such as authentication, cloud storage, infrastructure, security, or customer support providers. We may also disclose information where required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Service, maintain security, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. Encrypted backup information may remain stored until a user requests deletion or the account/service relationship ends, subject to legal obligations.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Your Choices

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of applicable personal information, subject to legal and operational limitations. Users may also discontinue use of the extension at any time.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at the same URL with a revised effective date.
